Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will became your new favorite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:

Fresch Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 11 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing the top
  • 10 unces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    in a medium-sized mizing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
